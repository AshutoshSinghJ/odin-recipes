Pasta

Description

We all love Pasta,it is an Italian cuisine which is famous worldwide Today we will make that.

Ingredients

  • Macroni or Baby Pasta
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • few drops of oil
  • Any extra veggies you want

Steps

  1. Fine chop Onion,garlic and Tomatoes
  2. Chop any extra veggies you want
  3. Add salt,garam masala,coriander powder and make a mixture
  4. Boil water,add 1 teaspoon salt,1.5 cup pasta and 1 tsp oil
  5. Cook the pasta on a medium flame roughly 10 minutes
  6. Cook until it is soft and not chewy
  7. Fry all the vegetables and add the spices
  8. Add cooked pasta and mix well,if it is dry add more water
  9. Sprinkle Coriander leaves and Serve pasta hot
