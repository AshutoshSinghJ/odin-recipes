Burger

Description

A Burger is an very easy food to make.

Ingredients

  • Two pieces of Bun
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Coriander leaves
  • 1 slice of Cheese
  • Little salt
  • few drops of oil
  • Any extra veggies you want
  • Potato

Steps

  1. Fry two buns in little oil
  2. Make a crispy pattie
  3. Fine slice Onion and Tomatoes
  4. Add a cheese slice and crispy pattie
  5. Chop any extra veggies you want
  6. Add little salt and add anything else you want
  7. Cook until it is Crispy
  8. Apply Tomato Sauce and Serve Burger hot
