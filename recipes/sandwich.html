Sandwich

Description

A sandwich is an very easy food to make.

Ingredients

  • Two pieces of Bread
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Coriander leaves
  • 1 slice of Cheese
  • Little salt
  • few drops of oil
  • Any extra veggies you want

Steps

  1. Fine slice Onion and Tomatoes
  2. Add a cheese slice
  3. Chop any extra veggies you want
  4. Add little salt and add anything else you want
  5. Cook the Sandwich on a low flame roughly 10 minutes or in Oven with little oil
  6. Cook until it is Crispy
  7. Apply Tomato Sauce and Serve Sandwich hot
